Section F - Student Assessment of Supervision, Technical and Administrative Support


*This section is to be sent to the Faculty Postgraduate Chair. It can be used for positive and/or negative comments.

It is very important that you identify and write down any concerns you have about your supervisor(s), technical or administrative staff even when you feel this may be inappropriate (e.g., for cultural reasons). You should not feel powerless to identify your concerns.

Information on this form is confidential to the Faculty Postgraduate Chair and will not automatically be shown to your supervisors, technical or administrative staff. However, we are not allowed by law to withhold these forms from people mentioned in them should they request them. For this reason, you may prefer to discuss the problem(s) instead of/before committing to it. The best way to prevent small problems becoming big ones is to discuss the Mutual Expectations Agreement document with your supervisor(s) as well as any incipient supervisor problems. If these routes fail, then the other options available to you are mentioned above.

The Faculty Postgraduate Chair will contact you to discuss any issues if you tick the box at the bottom of this form. Through this discussion the most appropriate course of subsequent action will be discussed with you. Unlike the form itself, the details of these oral discussions will remain confidential.

Always keep a copy of this form when completed, for your own record.
